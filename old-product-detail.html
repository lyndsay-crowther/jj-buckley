ROBERT PARKER'S WINE ADVOCATE, AUGUST 2018

The 2005 Chablis 1er Cru Côte de Léchet is more complex than the Vaillon, unfurl-ing with complex aromas of honeycomb, mandarin oil, fresh mushroom, almond paste and oyster shell. On the palate, it's full-bodied, rich and gourmand, with asatiny attack, a deep, concentrated core and tangy balancing acids. It's a textural, sapid Chablis that will work well at the table.
